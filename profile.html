Justin's personal work blends intimate experiences with broader themes through art and design, creating ideas that resonate universally. His diverse subjects reflect modern life's complexities, including popular culture, iconography, design systems, typography, nature, memories, and emotions.

His reflective approach explores contrasts: natural versus man-made, traditional versus modern, and historical versus contemporary. Through his art, he invites viewers to ponder these dichotomies, fostering a deeper understanding of our multifaceted world.
