The hardware and software you use on a daily basis — whether it's for work or a part of the hobby you love — matters. While it may not directly make you better, it does enhance the experience. And that's important. Hardware. Software. Gear. Aside from enhancing the experience, it can help make the effort easier and more enjoyable. All that said, perhaps the most important thing is to not let your affectations for the gear turn into an act of seduction or fetishization. These things are just supplemental to the real star of the show: you.

So, below is a non-exhaustive overview of what helps me to my job better, move through my day better, and check off those personal goals.
